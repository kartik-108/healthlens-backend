Urology Department Overview

The Urology Department at HealthLens Hospital provides specialized care for conditions affecting the urinary system and male reproductive organs. Our expert urologists combine advanced technology with compassionate care to diagnose and treat a wide range of conditions, from kidney stones to prostate diseases.

Our department is equipped with cutting-edge diagnostic and surgical facilities, ensuring patients receive accurate evaluation and effective treatments with minimal discomfort.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Advanced endoscopy and minimally invasive surgery.
  • Kidney stone removal and lithotripsy services.
  • Comprehensive prostate and bladder care.
  • Male infertility evaluation and treatments.
  • Uro-oncology services for bladder, kidney, and prostate cancers.

About Urology Science ⚕️

Urology is the medical specialty that deals with the urinary tract system in both men and women, and the reproductive system in men. It involves the study, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases related to the kidneys, ureters, bladder, urethra, and male reproductive organs.

With advancements in minimally invasive surgery and robotics, urology has evolved to offer effective treatments with shorter recovery times and fewer complications.

Causes of Urological Conditions ⚠️

Urological conditions may arise due to aging, infections, genetic disorders, dietary habits, sedentary lifestyles, and cancers. In men, prostate enlargement is a common cause of urinary symptoms. In both men and women, dehydration and poor diet can lead to kidney stones.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms include painful urination, blood in urine, frequent urination, urinary retention, incontinence, infertility, and pelvic pain. If you notice these, consult a urologist immediately. Early treatment can prevent severe complications.

Drinking adequate water, maintaining hygiene, and regular check-ups are essential preventive measures.

Treatment 🏥

Treatments include medications for infections and prostate enlargement, minimally invasive surgeries for kidney stones and tumors, reconstructive surgeries, and therapies for male infertility. For cancers, we provide surgical, medical, and radiation oncology support.
