Dermatology Department Overview

The Dermatology Department at HealthLens Hospital specializes in diagnosing, treating, and preventing disorders of the skin, hair, and nails. Our team of dermatologists manages conditions ranging from acne, eczema, and psoriasis to more complex skin cancers and cosmetic concerns.

We focus on evidence-based treatments, early diagnosis, preventive care, and patient education to ensure optimal skin health and overall well-being.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Advanced diagnostic labs for skin disorders, infections, and allergies.
  • Laser therapy and cosmetic dermatology treatments.
  • Phototherapy units for psoriasis and vitiligo.
  • Skin cancer screening and biopsy services.
  • Minimally invasive procedures for mole, wart, and cyst removal.

About Dermatology Science 🧴

Dermatology is the branch of medicine focused on the health of skin, hair, and nails. Dermatologists diagnose and manage a wide range of conditions including infections, inflammatory diseases, genetic disorders, and cosmetic concerns. Modern dermatology integrates medical, surgical, and cosmetic procedures to maintain skin health and appearance.

Causes of Skin Disorders ⚠️

Skin problems may arise from infections, allergies, genetic conditions, environmental factors, hormonal changes, stress, or autoimmune disorders. Poor hygiene, diet, and exposure to harmful chemicals or UV radiation can also contribute. Preventive care and early intervention are crucial to avoid complications.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms may include rashes, redness, itching, hair loss, nail changes, or suspicious moles. Patients should seek dermatological consultation promptly. Proper skincare, sun protection, hygiene, and a balanced diet are essential preventive measures.

Treatment 🏥

Treatment options depend on the type and severity of the skin disorder. These include topical and oral medications, phototherapy, laser treatments, minor surgical procedures, and cosmetic interventions. Dermatologists also provide guidance on lifestyle changes and skincare routines to maintain long-term skin health.
