Orthopedics Department 🦴

Comprehensive bone, joint, and musculoskeletal care for all ages, combining surgical and non-surgical expertise.

Orthopedics Department Overview

The Orthopedics Department at HealthLens Hospital specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of disorders of the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. Our orthopedic surgeons, physiotherapists, and specialists provide comprehensive care for fractures, arthritis, sports injuries, spinal disorders, and congenital deformities.

We emphasize minimally invasive techniques, modern surgical procedures, and personalized rehabilitation programs to restore mobility, relieve pain, and enhance quality of life.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Advanced orthopedic surgery suites for joint replacement, fracture repair, and minimally invasive procedures.
  • Physiotherapy and rehabilitation centers with modern equipment.
  • Imaging services including X-ray, MRI, CT scans, and bone densitometry.
  • Arthroscopy and sports medicine facilities for early intervention and recovery.
  • Pain management programs for chronic orthopedic conditions.

About Orthopedic Science 🦴

Orthopedics is the branch of medicine that focuses on the musculoskeletal system. It involves the study, diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of bone, joint, and soft tissue disorders. Orthopedic science integrates surgical and non-surgical treatments, advanced imaging, and therapeutic rehabilitation to ensure patients regain function and mobility.

Causes of Orthopedic Disorders ⚠️

Orthopedic disorders may occur due to trauma, sports injuries, degenerative diseases like arthritis, osteoporosis, infections, congenital defects, or lifestyle factors such as poor posture and repetitive strain. Early diagnosis and intervention are crucial for preventing long-term disability.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms can include joint pain, swelling, limited mobility, fractures, numbness, or muscle weakness. Patients experiencing these signs should consult an orthopedic specialist promptly. Rehabilitation, physiotherapy, and lifestyle modifications are essential components of effective management.

Treatment 🏥

Orthopedic treatments include medications, physical therapy, minimally invasive procedures, and surgical interventions such as joint replacement, fracture fixation, and arthroscopy. Post-surgery rehabilitation and pain management ensure optimal recovery and restoration of function.
