ENT Department 👂👃👄

Specialized care for Ear, Nose, and Throat disorders — hearing, breathing, and speech wellness.

ENT Department Overview

The ENT Department at HealthLens Hospital deals with diseases and disorders of the ear, nose, and throat. This includes hearing loss, sinus issues, tonsillitis, laryngitis, sleep apnea, and other related conditions. Our ENT specialists provide comprehensive care for children and adults using advanced diagnostic tools and treatment protocols.

With state-of-the-art audiology labs, endoscopic equipment, and surgical facilities, we ensure accurate diagnosis and effective management of ENT disorders.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Advanced audiology labs for hearing assessment and cochlear implant evaluation.
  • Endoscopic examination and microsurgery for nasal and throat disorders.
  • Sleep study and apnea management facilities.
  • Pediatric ENT clinics and allergy management programs.
  • Hearing aid fitting and rehabilitation services.

About ENT Science 👂👃👄

ENT (Otorhinolaryngology) is the branch of medicine focusing on disorders of the ear, nose, throat, and related structures. Healthy ENT function is crucial for hearing, breathing, speech, and balance. ENT specialists diagnose, manage, and treat these conditions to maintain overall health and quality of life.

Causes of ENT Disorders ⚠️

ENT problems may arise due to infections, allergies, trauma, genetic factors, environmental pollutants, or age-related degeneration. Conditions like sinusitis, otitis media, hearing loss, tonsillitis, and sleep apnea are common and need timely evaluation.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms include hearing loss, ear pain, dizziness, nasal congestion, snoring, sore throat, or difficulty swallowing. Patients experiencing these should consult an ENT specialist promptly. Early diagnosis can prevent complications and improve outcomes.

Treatment 🏥

Treatments include medications, hearing aids, endoscopic procedures, microsurgeries, allergy management, speech therapy, and in severe cases, surgical interventions for structural issues. Post-treatment rehabilitation and follow-up are essential for complete recovery.
