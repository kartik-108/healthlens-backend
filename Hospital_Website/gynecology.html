Gynecology Department 🤰

Specialized care for women's health, pregnancy, and reproductive wellness.

Gynecology Department Overview

The Gynecology Department at HealthLens Hospital focuses on the health of the female reproductive system, including menstruation, fertility, pregnancy, childbirth, and menopause. Our specialists provide comprehensive care for gynecologic disorders such as PCOS, fibroids, endometriosis, menstrual irregularities, and reproductive planning.

We combine advanced diagnostic tools, minimally invasive surgeries, prenatal and postnatal care, and personalized health programs to ensure women receive the highest standard of care throughout every stage of life.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Advanced diagnostic labs for reproductive health, ultrasounds, and hormone testing.
  • Minimally invasive surgery suites for laparoscopy, hysteroscopy, and gynecologic surgeries.
  • Comprehensive prenatal and postnatal care programs.
  • Fertility evaluation, counseling, and assisted reproductive technologies.
  • Menopause management and women’s wellness clinics.

About Gynecology Science 🤰

Gynecology is the medical practice specializing in women's reproductive health, including the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disorders affecting the uterus, ovaries, fallopian tubes, and breasts. Gynecologists focus on overall female wellness and reproductive life planning.

Causes of Gynecologic Disorders ⚠️

Gynecologic disorders can arise due to hormonal imbalances, infections, genetic conditions, lifestyle factors, pregnancy complications, and age-related changes. Common conditions include PCOS, fibroids, endometriosis, and menstrual irregularities.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms include irregular periods, pelvic pain, abnormal bleeding, fertility issues, and menopausal symptoms. Women experiencing these should consult a gynecologist promptly. Early detection and regular checkups are essential for maintaining reproductive health.

Treatment 🏥

Treatments include medications, hormonal therapy, minimally invasive surgeries, fertility treatments, prenatal and postnatal care, lifestyle modifications, and counseling. Personalized care plans ensure optimal health and well-being at every stage of a woman's life.
