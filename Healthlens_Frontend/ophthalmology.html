Ophthalmology Department 👁️

Specialized care for eye health and vision — seeing the world clearly with expert ophthalmologists.

Ophthalmology Department Overview

The Ophthalmology Department at HealthLens Hospital focuses on diagnosing, treating, and managing eye and vision disorders. This includes cataracts, glaucoma, refractive errors, retinal diseases, diabetic retinopathy, and age-related macular degeneration.

We combine advanced diagnostics, surgical expertise, and comprehensive vision care programs to maintain and restore optimal eye health for patients of all ages.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Advanced diagnostic tools including OCT and fundus photography.
  • Cataract and refractive surgery suites with modern laser systems.
  • Glaucoma and retinal clinics for specialized care.
  • Pediatric ophthalmology and vision therapy programs.
  • Low vision rehabilitation and corneal transplant facilities.

About Ophthalmology Science 👁️

Ophthalmology is the branch of medicine dealing with the anatomy, physiology, and diseases of the eye. Ophthalmologists diagnose, treat, and prevent eye disorders to ensure healthy vision and overall quality of life.

Causes of Eye Disorders ⚠️

Eye problems may result from aging, genetics, infections, trauma, lifestyle factors, or systemic diseases like diabetes and hypertension. Early detection and treatment are crucial to prevent vision loss.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms include blurred vision, eye pain, redness, floaters, flashes of light, and sudden vision changes. Patients experiencing these signs should consult an ophthalmologist promptly.

Treatment 🏥

Treatments include prescription glasses or contact lenses, medications, laser therapy, cataract and refractive surgeries, retinal procedures, glaucoma management, and comprehensive follow-up care.
