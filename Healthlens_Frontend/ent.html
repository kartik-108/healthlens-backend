ENT Department Overview

The ENT Department at HealthLens Hospital deals with diseases and disorders of the ear, nose, and throat. This includes hearing loss, sinus issues, tonsillitis, laryngitis, sleep apnea, and other related conditions.

With state-of-the-art audiology labs, endoscopic equipment, and surgical facilities, we ensure accurate diagnosis and effective management of ENT disorders.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Advanced audiology labs
  • Endoscopic examination and microsurgery
  • Sleep study and apnea management
  • Pediatric ENT clinics
  • Hearing aid fitting services

About ENT Science 👂👃👄

ENT (Otorhinolaryngology) focuses on disorders of the ear, nose, throat, and related structures that affect hearing, breathing, speech, and balance.

Causes of ENT Disorders ⚠️

ENT problems may arise due to infections, allergies, trauma, genetic factors, pollutants, or age-related degeneration.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms include hearing loss, ear pain, dizziness, nasal congestion, sore throat, or difficulty swallowing.

Treatment 🏥

Treatments include medications, hearing aids, endoscopic procedures, microsurgeries, allergy management, and speech therapy.
