Gynecology Department Overview

The Gynecology Department at HealthLens Hospital focuses on the health of the female reproductive system, including menstruation, fertility, pregnancy, childbirth, and menopause.

We combine advanced diagnostics, minimally invasive surgeries, prenatal and postnatal care, and personalized health programs.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Advanced reproductive health diagnostics
  • Minimally invasive gynecologic surgeries
  • Prenatal and postnatal care programs
  • Fertility evaluation and counseling
  • Menopause and wellness clinics

About Gynecology Science 🤰

Gynecology specializes in women's reproductive health and overall wellness through every life stage.

Causes of Gynecologic Disorders ⚠️

Disorders may arise due to hormonal imbalance, infections, genetics, pregnancy complications, or aging.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms include irregular periods, pelvic pain, abnormal bleeding, and fertility issues.

Treatment 🏥

Treatment includes medications, hormonal therapy, surgery, fertility treatments, and comprehensive maternity care.
