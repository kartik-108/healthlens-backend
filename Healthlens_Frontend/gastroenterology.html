Gastroenterology Department Overview

The Gastroenterology Department at HealthLens Hospital is dedicated to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of digestive system disorders.

Using modern diagnostic technologies and advanced endoscopic procedures, we ensure accurate diagnosis and effective treatment tailored to each patient.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Advanced endoscopy and colonoscopy units
  • ERCP for bile duct and pancreatic conditions
  • State-of-the-art imaging services
  • Liver function and hepatitis testing labs
  • Pediatric gastroenterology clinics

About Gastro Science 🍎

Gastroenterology focuses on the digestive tract and its associated organs responsible for digestion and nutrient absorption.

Causes of Digestive Disorders ⚠️

Digestive problems may result from poor diet, stress, infections, alcohol use, smoking, genetic factors, or autoimmune conditions.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms include abdominal pain, bloating, nausea, vomiting, weight loss, jaundice, or changes in bowel habits.

Treatment 🏥

Treatments include dietary changes, medications, lifestyle modification, endoscopic procedures, or surgery depending on the condition.
