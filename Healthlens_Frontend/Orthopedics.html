Orthopedics Department Overview

The Orthopedics Department at HealthLens Hospital specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation of disorders of the musculoskeletal system, including bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles.

We emphasize minimally invasive techniques, modern surgical procedures, and personalized rehabilitation programs to restore mobility, relieve pain, and enhance quality of life.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Advanced orthopedic surgery suites for joint replacement and fracture repair.
  • Physiotherapy and rehabilitation centers with modern equipment.
  • Imaging services including X-ray, MRI, CT scans, and bone densitometry.
  • Arthroscopy and sports medicine facilities.
  • Pain management programs for chronic orthopedic conditions.

About Orthopedic Science 🦴

Orthopedics focuses on the musculoskeletal system, integrating surgical and non-surgical treatments, advanced imaging, and therapeutic rehabilitation to ensure patients regain function and mobility.

Causes of Orthopedic Disorders ⚠️

Disorders may arise from trauma, sports injuries, degenerative diseases like arthritis, osteoporosis, infections, congenital defects, or lifestyle factors such as poor posture.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms include joint pain, swelling, fractures, limited mobility, numbness, or muscle weakness. Early consultation with an orthopedic specialist is essential.

Treatment 🏥

Treatments include medication, physiotherapy, minimally invasive procedures, and surgeries such as joint replacement and arthroscopy, followed by rehabilitation.
