Pediatrics Department 🧒

Comprehensive child healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents, combining preventive, diagnostic, and treatment services.

Pediatrics Department Overview

The Pediatrics Department at HealthLens Hospital focuses on the overall health and well-being of children from birth to adolescence. Our team provides preventive care, routine check-ups, immunizations, growth monitoring, and specialized treatments.

By combining evidence-based medicine with compassionate care, we support families with nutrition guidance, education, and wellness planning.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Child-friendly consultation and examination rooms.
  • Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU).
  • Advanced pediatric diagnostic labs.
  • Vaccination and immunization programs.
  • Growth, nutrition, and developmental monitoring.

About Pediatric Science 🧬

Pediatrics focuses on growth, development, disease prevention, immunization, nutrition, and promoting healthy habits from early childhood.

Causes of Pediatric Illnesses ⚠️

Illnesses may arise from infections, genetic conditions, allergies, nutritional deficiencies, and environmental factors. Preventive care is essential.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms include fever, rash, vomiting, breathing difficulty, or abnormal growth. Parents should seek medical care if symptoms persist.

Treatment 🏥

Treatment includes medications, therapies, surgeries when needed, and long-term growth monitoring with family education.
