Neurology Department Overview

The Neurology Department at HealthLens Hospital focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves, and muscles. Our neurologists provide care for epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson’s disease, neuropathies, migraines, and neurodegenerative conditions.

Using advanced imaging, electrodiagnostic tests, and personalized treatment plans, we aim to improve neurological function, prevent complications, and enhance patients’ quality of life.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Advanced neuroimaging: MRI, CT, PET scans.
  • EEG, EMG, and nerve conduction studies.
  • Stroke unit with thrombolytic therapy and rehabilitation.
  • Neurocritical care and intensive monitoring.
  • Specialized clinics for epilepsy, Parkinson’s, and headaches.

About Neurology Science 🧠

Neurology is the branch of medicine that deals with disorders of the nervous system. Neurologists diagnose, treat, and manage conditions affecting the brain, spinal cord, nerves, and muscles using clinical evaluation, imaging, and electrophysiology.

Causes of Neurological Disorders ⚠️

Causes include genetic factors, infections, vascular diseases, trauma, neurodegenerative conditions, autoimmune disorders, and metabolic imbalances. Timely diagnosis and management are crucial.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms may include headaches, seizures, weakness, numbness, difficulty speaking, memory loss, tremors, or balance problems. Prompt neurological consultation is recommended.

Treatment 🏥

Treatments include medications, lifestyle modifications, physical and occupational therapy, surgical interventions, and neurorehabilitation. Personalized care improves outcomes and quality of life.
