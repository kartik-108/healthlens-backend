Pulmonology Department Overview

The Pulmonology Department at HealthLens Hospital focuses on diseases and disorders of the lungs and respiratory system.

We provide advanced diagnostics, pulmonary testing, and personalized treatment plans.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Advanced pulmonary function and spirometry testing.
  • Bronchoscopy and interventional pulmonology services.
  • High-resolution imaging for lung evaluation.
  • Ventilator and critical respiratory care support.
  • Smoking cessation, asthma, and COPD programs.

About Pulmonology Science 🌬️

Pulmonology deals with diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of respiratory system diseases.

Causes of Respiratory Disorders ⚠️

Causes include infections, smoking, allergies, pollution, and chronic lung diseases.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms include breathlessness, chronic cough, wheezing, and chest discomfort.

Treatment 🏥

Treatment includes inhalers, medications, oxygen therapy, pulmonary rehab, and advanced procedures.
