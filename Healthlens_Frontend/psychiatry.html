Psychiatry Department 🧠

Comprehensive mental health care with expert psychiatrists, therapy, and counseling services.

Psychiatry Department Overview

The Psychiatry Department at HealthLens Hospital provides specialized care for mental health disorders, emotional well-being, and cognitive functioning.

We combine evidence-based therapies, medication management, counseling, and holistic approaches to support mental wellness.

Department Facilities 🏥

  • Individual and group therapy sessions.
  • Medication management and psychiatric consultations.
  • Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT).
  • Stress management and mindfulness programs.
  • Support groups and family counseling.

About Psychiatry Science 🧠

Psychiatry focuses on diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental, emotional, and behavioral disorders.

Causes of Psychiatric Disorders ⚠️

Disorders may arise from genetic, biological, environmental, or psychological factors including stress and trauma.

Symptoms and What to Do 🩺

Symptoms include anxiety, mood swings, hallucinations, sleep disturbances, and withdrawal. Early consultation is advised.

Treatment 🏥

Treatment includes psychotherapy, CBT, medication, lifestyle changes, and structured support programs.
